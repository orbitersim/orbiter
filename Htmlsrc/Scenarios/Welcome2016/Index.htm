Welcome to Orbiter 2016!

The new Edition of Orbiter Space Flight Simulator has finally arrived. This version supports surface elevation, higher texture resolutions for planetary surfaces and cloud layers, more realistic visualisation of water surfaces and other visual improvements.

Under the hood, a redesigned physics engine drives the simulator, including improved surface impact modelling and timestep subsampling.

Some of the default spacecraft have received improvements to their instrument panels and subsystem design.

Launch this scenario for a pre-recorded tour of the highlights in this edition.
