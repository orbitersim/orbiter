Orbiter Challenges: 2

You are piloting a Delta-glider from low Earth orbit to rendezvous with a space station orbiting the moon.

The challenge is completed as soon as you dock with the Luna-OB1 lunar orbital station. Similar to Challenge 1, mission success is measured by the amount of fuel left in the tank.

You will have to plan your transfer orbit carefully to be as fuel-efficient as possible. The arrival at the moon and lunar orbit insertion should be planned so that a minimum of Delta-V is required for matching the orbital plane of the lunar orbital station. Approach, rendezvous and docking at Luna-OB1 should of course also be performed with minimal fuel expenditure.

To randomize the challenge a bit, the start time of the scenario is set to the current time, so you may have to rethink you ejection and arrival times.

Good luck!
