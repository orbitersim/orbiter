Earth views 2016

A flyby over some of Earth's surface formations in Orbiter 2016, featuring new textures and elevation maps.

Note that the default Orbiter installation only comes with a low-resolution version of the Earth maps. To see the full supported resolution in Orbiter as featured in the screenshots below, you need to install the full resolution maps. Go to the Orbiter home page for links and installation instructions. Be sure to have plenty of free space on your hard drive!
