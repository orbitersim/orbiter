COM/NAV MFD controls

Keyboard Button Description
Shift , SL- Select previous NAV receiver/transmitter.
Shift . SL+ Select next NAV receiver/transmitter.
Shift - << Step down frequency by 1 kHz.
Shift = >> Step up frequency by 1 kHz.
Shift [ < Step down frequency by 0.05 kHz.
Shift ] > Step up frequency by 0.05 kHz.
