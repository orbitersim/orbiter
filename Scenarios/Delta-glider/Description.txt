# Delta-glider scenarios

The Delta-glider is a futuristic spaceplane design with aerodynamic control
surfaces and powerful engines. The DG is a capable allround vessel ideally
suited for novice pilots to practise orbital manoeuvres.

This version has been re-designed and improved, and now includes a variant with
air-breathing scramjet engines. The glider is also the first craft to support a
full virtual cockpit implementation.
